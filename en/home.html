Welcome to our services!

With our competence and enthusiasm we want to contribute to making you feel handsome or beautiful and content.
We are hairdressers with our hearts in the trade.

Our specialty is to offer you a complete service package: you can experience a comfortable break, let your soul dangle, feel spoiled by us and enjoy thorough advice for your hair and style.
However, when it needs to be quick and effective, then we’re also the right salon to cut or trim your contours.

It would be best if you set up your appointment through a message to our cell phone or by selecting an open time slot in our online booking system. The definite advantage to the online software solution is that you can reach us quickly and easily and that we can stay with our customers that we are currently serving. We enjoy concentrating on our trade skills and want to understand your needs and advise you accordingly.

For telephone calls please consider that I am hard of hearing and that you must speak loudly and clearly into the telephone so that I can understand you optimally.


Our Team

When you come to us, we offer you a classic selection of services in the salon. We would like to create a content atmosphere especially for you with advice, haircuts, styling, color, hair makeovers and small cosmetic extras. We work together as a team and complement each other with particular specialty areas:

Beatrice

My passion is the stylistic preparation for events as a complete package. Braided elements are a special part of my repertoire.
My haircuts are classic and neat, and, if desired, they can have modern accents with that special something that can highlight your personal type.

Selda

I’m your expert for color changes! The more challenging your color preference the more I enjoy pleasing your selection – how about a color makeover? It doesn’t matter if you want sun-kissed highlights, a glossy brunette tone, a popping red shade or radiant blond hair, together we’ll find your tone and hairstyle.

We’ll serve you as a team according to your desire or idea.
